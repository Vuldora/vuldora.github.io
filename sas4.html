Timeskip exploits

Nightmare ticket reset

Platforms: Android.
Description: this exploit lets you skip the waiting time for nightmare tickets and instantly gain 5 new daily tickets.
Steps to reproduce:
1. Enable splitscreen and have the settings app in a floating window.
2. Click multiplayer and change the date to be one day ahead.
3. Go back to the main menu and repeat step 2 until your tickets have been reset.

Singleplayer contract timeskip

Platforms: Android, PC, IOS.
Description: allows you to skip ahead to the desired contract.
Steps to reproduce:
1. Disconnect from internet.
2. Change time to be ahead by 20-30 minutes.
3. Repeat until you find the contract you want, and note it down for future use.

Multiplayer contract timeskip

Platforms: Android, PC.
Description: allows you to skip ahead to the desired contract.
Steps to reproduce (PC):
1. Go to the contract match screen (showing Single Player, Quick Match, etc).
2. Disconnect from internet and change date/time to the contract you want.
3. Exit back to your character select screen.
4. Return to the contract match screen.
5. Change date/time 1 minute forward.
6. Reconnect internet.
7. Click "create match". You should see the contract you timeskipped to
8. If you want to play multiple contracts in a row, disconnect internet during the match and you should be able to continue from Step 4. (if not then you will need to restart from step 1).



Steps to reproduce (Android):
1. Click contracts.
2. Change date/time.
3. Create Private Match and play.
* To play multiple contracts in a row without changing date/time after every game: 4. Disconnect from wifi before match ends.
5. Click contracts.
6. Reconnect wifi.
7. Repeat 3-7.

Faction armory timeskip

Platforms: Android, PC, IOS.
Description: allows you to skip ahead or back in time to buy a desired armory item. Steps to reproduce:
1. Change date and time.
2. Show faction member screen.
3. Turn off internet.
4. Wait a few seconds for the game to read local time.
5. Click armory button, the game will automatically return to faction member screen. 6. Turn on internet.
7. Click armory button immediately after connecting to the internet, this makes the game use the local time instead of server time.
8. Retry from step 2 if it fails and be quicker.

Leaving Factions

Platforms: Android, PC, IOS.
Description: lets you leave a faction you're currently in.
Steps to reproduce:
1. Select the elimination or terminals planet.
2. Turn off internet.
3. Start the match.
4. Change the date 3 days in the future on your device.
5. Be killed by zombies or radiation.
6. Wait for the mission complete screen.
7. Turn on internet.
8. Select OK button on the mission complete screen.

Early event rewards

Platforms: Android, PC.
Description: after getting a good score in events you can force the game to give out the rewards early which means you don't have to compete with later scores.
Steps to reproduce:
1. Force backup after getting to your desired reward level.
2. Go to your character select screen and repeatedly change your date/time 1 day forward. You may need to do this numerous times, but it should eventually work.
3. Once you get the event reward popup, click "collect reward" and quickly mash on any of your characters. If you successfully open a character, force backup; if not, close and re-open the game and go back to step 2.
4. After forcing a backup go back to character select screen and claim event rewards normally.

Rerolling exploits

Faction armory reroll

Platforms: Android, PC, IOS.
Description: Whenever you join a new faction, the items inside the boxes offered in the armory change, the goal is to keep joining factions and opening boxes until you get the desired item.
Steps to reproduce:
1. Force backup
2. Join a new faction and buy a strongbox
3. If you don't get the item(s) you want, close the game and delete your save; on PC delete all 3 profile.save files, on Android clear cache, on IOS reinstall the game.
4. Reopen game, repeat steps until you get the item you want.

Events reward reroll

Platforms: Android, PC, IOS.
Description: Allows you to reroll items from event boxes.
Steps to reproduce:
1. Force backup after playing your last game of the event and close game
2. Once the event ends, open the game and collect rewards
3. If you don't get the item(s) you want, close the game and delete your save; on PC delete all 3 profile.save files, on Android clear cache, on IOS reinstall the game. 4. Reopen game, repeat steps until you get the item you want.

Global event reroll

Platforms: Android, PC, IOS.
Description: Allows you to reroll items from global events boxes.
Steps to reproduce:
1. Force backup before the strongbox milestone is reached, and close the game
2. Once the GE ends, open the game and go to any character. Exit to character select screen and collect rewards
3. If you don't get the item(s) you want, close the game and delete your save; on PC delete all 3 profile.save files, on Android clear cache, on IOS reinstall the game. 4. Reopen game, repeat steps until you get the item you want.

Misc exploits

Bugged/private factions

Platforms: Android, PC, IOS.
Description: Private factions are factions where only people with the code are able to participate in faction warfare, meaning only one team in the war has players and the rest are empty, this ensures the quick and permanent capture of all planets.
Steps to reproduce:
1. If already in a faction, wait for the current war to end.
2. In-game go to multiplayer then private match, click create and choose any map.
3. On screen you should see a match invite code, write down the first three letters.
4. Go back to the main screen then select faction warfare.
5. In the play with friends field type the three letters from before, followed by three random letters, and a number from 0 to 7. For example if the three letters were XYZ try XYZABC1. You'll likely get an error message, keep trying different codes with the same initial letters until you join a faction.

Bugged terminals

Platforms: Android, PC, IOS.
Description: This makes matches never end even if all players are dead, allowing players to grind credits as long as they wish. I believe this exploit works in other gamemodes as well but I haven't tested that yet.
Steps to reproduce:
1- Have another player create a terminal match.
2- Open the game on two different devices and pick a different character on each.
3- Join the private match on both devices.
4- Now you can AFK while the rest of the players run from terminal to terminal
5- Disconnect when you want to end the match.
